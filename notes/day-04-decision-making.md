# Day 4 – Decision Making

## What I Learned

Today I learned that software makes decisions using conditions.

The computer does not think on its own. It evaluates rules written by the programmer.

## HealthTech Connection

Hospital systems use conditions constantly.

Examples:

- Is the patient registered?
- Is the patient over 18?
- Does the patient have a fever?
- Has payment been confirmed?
- Is the laboratory sample available?

Understanding conditions is an important step toward building intelligent healthcare software.

## Reflection

Today was the first time I realized that programming is about solving real-world problems using logic.
